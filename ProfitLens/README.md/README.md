ProfitLens: Retail Sales \& Profit Intelligence Dashboard



Overview



ProfitLens is an interactive business intelligence dashboard developed using Power BI, Excel, and DAX to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.



The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making.





Business Problem



Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify performance drivers and profitability trends.



This project aims to answer:



* &#x20;Which categories generate the highest revenue?
* &#x20;Which categories are most profitable?
* &#x20;Which regions contribute the most sales and profit?
* &#x20;Which products and customers drive business performance?
* &#x20;How does sales performance change over time?

Key Insights



Category Performance



* &#x20;Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* &#x20;Furniture generated strong revenue but comparatively lower profitability.



&#x20;Regional Performance



* &#x20;West region delivered the highest sales and profit.
* &#x20;South region generated the lowest sales performance.



&#x20;Product Analysis



* &#x20;Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of high-performing products.
* &#x20;A small group of customers contributes significantly to total sales.



&#x20;Profitability



* &#x20;Overall profit margin reached 12.47%.
* &#x20;Significant variation exists across categories and sub-categories.
